About Us!


SafeNest was established in 2020 to assist street animals who have very few people to care for them in this big world. About 1,70,000 stray dogs and cats wander the country, with the number steadily increasing due to a lack of spay and neuter programmes to keep the population under control.
SafeNest was developed to provide a humane and long-term solution for handling and meeting the needs of the stray community. Funding then, as it does now, came entirely from individuals who shared, and continue to share, the vision of our founders.
Our mission is to provide medical care, shelter, and adoption to stray animals in need, as well as to give a proper send off to the animals who's lives has been expired . "To improve the health of stray dogs and other livestock in India, resulting in better lives for both animal and human populations, to establish a world without abandoned animals, and to eventually end animal cruelty," says the mission statement.
